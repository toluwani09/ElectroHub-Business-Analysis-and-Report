# 📊 ElectroHub Business Intelligence & Sales Analysis

## 🧠 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Business Intelligence analysis of ElectroHub’s sales data, with the goal of uncovering actionable insights to support strategic decision-making.

While initial performance metrics suggested strong revenue generation, deeper analysis revealed underlying structural issues affecting long-term business sustainability.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
The analysis was designed to address key business questions:

- What are the top and bottom performing products by sales, profit, and quantity?
- How have sales trends evolved over time?
- What is the relationship between sales and profit?
- How does performance compare across different time periods?
- How effective are discount and promotion strategies?
- What trends exist in customer purchasing behavior (orders)?
- How do sales vary across different locations?

✅ Product Strategy
Top Performers:
Laptop Pro 15 and Smartphone X200 account for 38% of total revenue over the past 6 months.
Recommendation: Maintain stock levels for these high-demand products and explore complementary accessories (e.g., laptop bags, phone cases) to increase average basket size by 5–7%.
Low Performers:
Wireless Mouse Basic, USB-C Hub, and Tablet Cover Standard collectively contribute <2% of revenue each and have >30% unsold inventory rate.
Recommendation: Consider phasing out or bundling these with higher-performing products to improve turnover and reduce storage costs.
✅ Pricing & Revenue Mix
Pricing Insights:
Smartphone X200 generates 15% profit margin at current pricing; Laptop Pro 15 is 28% margin.
Recommendation: Introduce tiered pricing or bundle offers (e.g., Smartphone X200 + Earbuds) to increase revenue per transaction by approx. ₦5,000–₦7,000.
Revenue Diversification:
Currently, top 5 products contribute 55% of revenue, leaving middle-tier products underutilized.
Recommendation: Promote mid-tier products (Laptop Pro 13, Smartphone Z50) via targeted campaigns to balance revenue mix.
✅ Promotion Optimization
Promotion Performance:
Holiday Discount 10% and Buy 1 Get 1 Free (USB-C Hub) accounted for 70% of sales uplift, but the ROI for the USB-C Hub promotion was -8% due to low sell-through.
Recommendation: Limit extreme discounts to high-demand products; standardize discount range to max 15%.
Recommendation: Use ROI ≥ 20% as the baseline for future campaigns, tracked per product category.
✅ Customer Retention
Customer Insights:
Top 20% of customers (based on purchase frequency) account for 62% of revenue, while 35% of customers made only 1 purchase in the last 6 months.
Recommendation: Launch loyalty programs targeting repeat customers (e.g., 5% off on second purchase of Laptop Pro or Smartphone X200) to increase repeat purchase rate by 10–12% over the next quarter.
Segmentation:
Segment customers by product preference:
Electronics Enthusiasts → mostly laptops & phones
Accessory Buyers → mostly chargers, hubs, covers
Tailor marketing campaigns to each segment for better engagement.
